About Us

We are INFI SPORTS, dedicated to bringing together sports enthusiasts from all backgrounds...

Founded by College Students, Built for Competitors

We organize and stream well-structured and action-packed sports tournaments, starting off in Chennai’s southern province.

What We Offer:

  • Competitive Tournaments – Organized and fair play for all
  • Live Streaming – Quality video content shot for all gameplay
  • Family-Friendly Environment – With all essential amenities
  • Entry for all – Guaranteed quality game time for all participants

What We Organize:

  • 3x3 Street Basketball Tournaments
  • 5x5 & 7x7 Turf Football Leagues
  • Single & Doubles Corporate Badminton Leagues
  • Men’s, Women’s & Mixed Doubles Championships
  • Indoor & Outdoor Volleyball Leagues
  • Singles & Doubles Open Table Tennis Championships

We plan to launch more domestic leagues on INFI SPORTS once market research confirms demand for each sport.

Our first-ever tournament is not just about winning a trophy; it’s about winning for a cause! Funds raised will be donated to improve IT infrastructure in Tamil Nadu's government schools.

Every participant is a winner, as you are a part of something bigger than just a game!
